Worm Powers

A PRT guide to understanding Parahumans

Report authored by Watchdog for the PRT

60 years ago, the Golden Man aka Scion showed up hovering over the Atlantic Ocean. first contact noted that he was "extremely sad" like he had exxperienced a great loss. We now know what exactly this came to be after the events of Gold Morning two weeks ago, contained in a brief codename ABX-32QJ

Scion is believed to tbe the source of powers, and it was immediately after his appearance over the Atlantic that humans started manifesting abilities.

The PRT has over the years managed to classify these into 12 groups:

  • Movers
  • Shakers
  • Masters
  • Strangers
  • Thinkers
  • Tinkers
  • Brutes
  • Breakers
  • Changers
  • Trumps
  • Strikers
  • Blasters
